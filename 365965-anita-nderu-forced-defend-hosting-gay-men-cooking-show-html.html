Anita Nderu forced to defend herself after hosting gay men on her cooking show

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, March 21, 2024

- Anita Nderu was forced to explain herself after many Kenyans called her out on her latest cooking video

- The beauty hosted gay men and Kenyans were not pleased with the outcome with the majority blaming Pwani Life which manufactures Fresh Fri for allowing such a marketing strategy

- The company defended itself saying it had not contracted Nderu to market its product and apologised to its clients

- Many netizens were angered by the vocal media personality for sexualising the cooking show with the majority saying they never see her the same again

Vocal media personality Anita Nderu has found herself on the wrong side with netizens after hosting gay men in her cooking show.

Taking to the various social media platforms, her followers expressed their disappointment in the woman they said they valued highly.

After the video went viral, the beauty was forced to explain herself claiming those were just her friends who decided to show their real selves.

Responding to an Instagram fan identified as @whoisqueen, the beauty said that nothing about her show was scripted.

"I respect your opinion. I simply asked my friends to come on my web show and be themselves. What any of us have chosen to do on the show is how each of us is. Nothing about my show is scripted. What you see from the guests, food to outcome is what actually genuinely took place," responded Nderu.

However, the company behind the oil issued a statement denying having any deal with Nderu.
