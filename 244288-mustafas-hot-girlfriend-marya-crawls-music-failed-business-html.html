Mustafas hot ex-girlfriend Marya crawls back to music after failed business

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, May 3, 2024

- Popular Kenyan songstress Marya has announced her long-awaited comeback

- The singer will be making her way back into the music industry after a long hiatus

- Marya had ventured into businesses which did not do quite well, hence her return

Revered Kenyan songstress Marya of the ‘Chokoza’ fame will be making a gallant comeback into music if her recent revelation is anything to go by.

Marya, who used to be rapper Colonel Mustafa’s girlfriend took a long hiatus from music to concentrate with other things, among them pregnancy and businesses.

Her fans missed her a lot judging by thousands of comments on her social media pages.

Sparking a bout of excitement amongst her fans,Marya recently announced that she will be making a comeback into the music industry through an interview with a local entertainment site.

The singer, famed for the hit song ‘Chokoza’ in which she featured Avril said that she is about to reclaim her throne as far as music is concerned.

‘’I ventured into business which took a lot of my time and energy. I imported stuff for sale and also had an M-Pesa business but it did not work out. Doing business was not easy but now I am focusing on my music and family.’’Marya revealed.
