The deceased's body was later identified by his brother Michael Amollo on Thursday, April 29, who said that the body had no visible injuries.

"The body does not have any visible injuries, although we still don't know who booked in the body at the facility,” he said.

According to Daily Nation, Elijah Obuong, 35, Brian Oduor, 36, Jack Ochieng, 37, and Benjamin Imbai, 30, were last seen on April 19 while having lunch together at a popular nyama choma joint, a Kitengela club.

TUKO .co.ke had reported that the quartet's mobile phones went off at around 2pm on the fateful day, and since then, their whereabouts remained a puzzle.

Their families said they were all working in the car hire business, raising concerns that a deal gone south could have led to their disappearance.

The families also said a mysterious man called Erico reached out to them via social media and told them the location of a white Toyota Mark X car, which they were using on the fateful day.
