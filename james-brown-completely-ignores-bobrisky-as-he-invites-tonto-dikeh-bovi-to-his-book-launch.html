James Brown Completely Ignores Bobrisky, As He Invites Tonto Dikeh, Bovi And Other Celebrities To Hi

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, May 3, 2024

Nigerian crossdresser James Obialor popularly called James Brown is obviously giving his mentor and colleague Bobrisky, a run for his money with his latest achievements.

For some days now, James Brown has been speaking about his most anticipated and big book launch set to hold in June.

James Brown took to his Instagram page to share a video of some of the people whose attendance has been confirmed which includes Tonto Dikeh, Nigerian comedian Bovi, Papaya Ex, among others.

In the video, James Brown seemed excited about meeting some of these A-list celebrities and would be hosting them at his book launch.

Meanwhile, James Brown has ignored Bobrisky and considering their feud for the past few months and constant throwing of shades his mentor may not be at the event.

Amidst the loggerheads between the duo, it’s unarguably that Bobrisky was very instrumental in the growth of James Brown and his rise to fame.
