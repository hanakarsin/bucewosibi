Davido's Daughter, Imade Hospitalized - [Video]

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

Imade, the first daughter of Nigerian musician, David Adeleke, better known as Davido, has been hospitalized.

Naija News reports that Imade’s mother, Sophia Momodu confirmed this on Friday via her social media.

The socialite and influencer shared a video clip on her Snapchat today praying on her daughter as she walked her through a restroom in the hospital with drips attached to her hands.

Sharing the video, Sophia captioned, “One thing about the devil…he is gonna tell a lie, but we move”.

Naija News reports that Sophia is the first baby mama of Davido.

Meanwhile, Davido’s second baby mama, Amanda recently shared some personal details with her fans on social media.

Speaking via her Instagram story, the young mother answered some questions including one that was about her daughter, Hailey Adeleke’s wellbeing.

When asked how her daughter was feeling, she said the young girl was great but was feeling a little bit under the weather at that moment.

Her words; “She’s good, feeling a little under the weather today.”

Answering more questions, Amanda disclosed that being a mother can be quite challenging but she would not trade it for anything.

“It can be very challenging at times but wouldn’t trade it for anything else in this,” she said.

When asked about how co-parenting was with Davido she replied that it has been great.
