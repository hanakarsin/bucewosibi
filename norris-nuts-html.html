The Norris Nuts - Members, Ages, Trivia

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Monday, January 1, 0001

Family vlog channel starring surfer Sabre Norris and her siblings in a series of comedy vlogs, challenges and other entertaining videos. It has earned more than 7 million subscribers. 

Trivia

UnlistedLeaf was featured in a video on the channel called "Cake Challenge ft Unlistedleaf vs Norris Nuts." The family went on to produce other channels called NORRIS NUTS GAMING, NORRIS NUTS DO STUFF, NORRIS NUTS SQUAD and Norris Nuts Cooking. In 2021, they got a dog they named Winky. They also have a grandmother named Flizzy.
