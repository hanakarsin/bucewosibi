Davido Cancelled A Big Meeting With 'Oyibo Guys' Because Of Me Teni Shares Emotional Experience

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, June 21, 2024

Nigerian singer Teniola Apata popularly known as Teni has shared a very endearing experience she had with her colleague, Davido.

The entertainer who was a guest at N6’s podcast explained how Davido postponed a meeting because of her.

They were speaking on a range of topics that touched on the tragic demise of Davido’s son, Ifeanyi.

N6 said that he constantly puts David and Chioma in his prayers even though he cannot understand their grief because he has never had a child.

Teni then cuts in and says “Davido has been really good to me”.

She then recounts how he canceled an important meeting with some white men just to be there for the music video shoot of her song, For You.

Naija News recalls that David and his partner Chioma lost their three-year-old son Ifeanyi Adeleke in a drowning accident.

The duo were away and left him in the care of his nanny when the tragic incident occurred.

The lad was rushed to the hospital when he was found unconscious in the pool and was declared dead by the doctor on call.

David has been away from social media since the dire incident and has still remained silent on his 30th birthday which was yesterday, November, 21st.
