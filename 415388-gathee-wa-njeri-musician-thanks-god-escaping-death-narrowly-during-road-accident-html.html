Gathee Wa Njeri: Mugithi Musician Thanks God after Escaping Death Narrowly During Road Accident

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Saturday, June 22, 2024
  • Gathee, a Kikuyu musician, said his car was thrown over the bridge in the accident that occurred on Thursday, June 9, along Thika Road
  • The Mugithi star thanked God for saving his life in the deadly road crash that wrecked his vehicle completely
  • He said the road crash did not stop him from performing at two separate events over the weekend

Renowned Kikuyu musician Gathee wa Njeri has narrated how he cheated death on Thursday, June 9, during a grisly road accident on Thika Road.

Gathee said his car was thrown over the bridge on the fateful day and miraculously, he escaped unhurt.

In his heartfelt message on his Facebook page, the Mugithi star said the road crash did not stop him from performing at two separate events on Friday, June 11, and Saturday, June 12.

"On Thursday, June 10, I was involved in a tragic road accident along Thika Road at Ryosambu. I was thrown over the Bridge by a bus. By the grace of God, I Escaped unhurt(I was alone in the car)."Many have been calling me texting enquiring about my condition. I confess God works miracles, I am well," he said.

The benga musician thanked God for being alive and asked his followers to also praise God with him for saving him during the carnage.

"Through prayer and God's mercy, I am alive and given another chance. Let's continue praying for each other. God is there and He is powerful over all the other forces," he said.

In a separate report, a couple of months ago, Tanzanian singer Mr Nice was rushed to the hospital after being involved in a road accident in Kenya.

The Bongo flavour artiste cheated death after a lorry hit their small car that he and his friends were travelling in along the Naivasha highway.

The news about the legendary singer's accident was shared on Facebook by Tanzanian award-winning journalist Millard Ayo.

''The lorry was transporting milk to Nairobi when the driver lost control and his breaks failed to work. So he hit my car and we rolled. The glasses cut me deep with some getting into my body, my ear was also cut pretty bad but the doctors have managed to restore it,'' said Nice.
