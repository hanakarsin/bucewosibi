Comedian Henry Desagu Divulges Why He Does Not Post His Lover Online: &quot;Sio Wa Umati&quot;

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Sunday, February 11, 2024
  • A social media user inquired from Desagu why he does not post photos of his lover online
  • The comedian responded by pointing out that she is not public property
  • For years, Desagu has been linked to digital content creator Jackie Mbugua

Different celebrities employ different tactics when it comes to sharing their private life online. While some openly share photos of their partners online, others strive to keep them away at all costs.

Famous Kenyan comedian Henry Desagu falls into the latter category as he rarely posts his woman online.

This aspect came up on Instagram when one of his followers sought to find out why he does not post photos of his lover.

“Why haven't you posted photos of your wife online?” asked the netizens.

In his repose, Desagu pointed out that she is not public property.

"She is mine, not for the public," Desagu wrote.

Desagu’s woman

However, Desagu’s rumoured girlfriend Jackie Mbugua often shares photos with the comedian.

For instance, the last photo she posted with Desagu was on April 21, 2021.

In a photo he shared, Desagu was seen holding a goat and sheep atop a table while Akothee enjoyed her breakfast.

“I would like to apologise to Minicheps for apparently stealing your work and postings. Somebody tagged me on the photo, and I reposted it with no intention of stealing your intellectual properties. As a content creator, I value your work, and I am deeply sorry,” he wrote.
