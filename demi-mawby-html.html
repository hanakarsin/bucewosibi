Demi Rose Mawby - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, June 24, 2024

British model who quickly rose to fame working with WorldStar HipHop, FHM Magazine, M! Magazine Denmark and more. She also has a tremendous presence on Instagram, where she has over 19 million followers. She has been known for her lingerie photos and has promoted for the PrettyLittleThing fashion company.  

Before Fame

She dreamed of being a model even as a child and began her career when she applied to an agency and was contacted by them within 24 hours.

Trivia

She attended Wimbledon in 2017. She has also explored DJing as a career and created an account on SoundCloud. She has been featured on websites like The Daily Mail and Champagne and Shade. 

Family Life

She is from England and grew up with a sister. Her parents are Christine and Barrie Mawby. She once dated Tyga.

Associated With

She is friends and has worked with Alexandra Cane.
