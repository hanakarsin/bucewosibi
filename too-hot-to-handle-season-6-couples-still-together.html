Which Season 6 Too Hot to Handle Couples Are Still Together?

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, May 17, 2024

2 Hot 2 Stay Together 4 Cuffing Season? Which Season 6 'Too Hot to Handle' Couples Are Still Together?

Bad Lana being thrown into the mix Season 6 of 'Too Hot to Handle' made things even more difficult for the couples.

By Sheridan Singleton

Updated Aug. 2 2024, 10:13 a.m. ET

Who doesn't enjoy watching a show about hot young narcissists learning to love? That's precisely why the Netflix series Too Hot to Handle has had so much success over the years. The dating competion first premiered at the prime time for any new show to premiere: during the COVID-19 pandemic. The concept is unlike any other dating show, blending the Love Island structure but enforcing a high level of chastity. Similar to Love Is Blind, Too Hot To Handle is about looking beyond a pretty surface.

While it may not be a huge surprise that the show isn't very successful at creating long lasting connections, it has had a few wins. Season 1 couple Emily Miller and Cam Holmes welcomed a baby boy in June of 2024, while Season 4 couple Seb Melrose and Kayla Richart are also still together. Both couples broke up at some point before reconnecting, proving Lana is capable of teaching people how to love. Season 6 was as dramatic as ever, so which Too Hot To Handle couples are still together?

Bri Balram and Demari Davis win Season 6 of 'Too Hot to Handle.'

There was a bit of tit-for-tat in this couple. Bri and Demari were drawn to each other immediately before they each had moments of letting their hormones take control of their bodies. The first indiscretion came from Bri, who kissed another contestant and was sent to stay in what can only called a solitary confinement chamber. While in there, Demari got into his own trouble, thanks to Bad Lana. Despite both of their hiccups, they appear to still be together, but this hasn't been confirmed.

Flavia Laos Urbina and João Coronel split up with a break-up song.

Of all the couples, João and Flavia felt the most wholesome. The two had a fairly instant connection despite Flavia going with another contestant for the first date. Still, once they got together, it felt like these two were endgame material. Because of Flavia admitting to being attracted to one of the newcomers, João felt he couldn;t trust her to be faithful to him. So he split up with her ahead of the finale, with a forever infamous break-up song.

Katherine LaPrell and Charlie Jeer end the season together.

There is a perfect quote from RuPaul's Drag Race queen Scarlet Envy to sum up the pairing of Charlie and Katherine, and it's "Am I the drama?" Both Charlie and Katherine were the drama, as they both left Lucy and Louis in the dust after stringing them along on the show. Charlie bounced around between several women before coming back to Katherine. The duo got together, but are they still together? Judging by Instagram, it doesn't look like they lasted beyond the retreat. But hey, wilder things have happened.

Chris and Sabrina get together later in the season on 'Too Hot to Handle.'

Sabrina is one of two grenades who show up to shake up the house on Too Hot to Handle. Instead, she falls for Chris. Or, is it the other way around? Either way, they are super into each other all the way to the Season 6 finale. Outside of the show, they follow each other on Instagram, but no confirmed word yet on if they're still dating IRL.
