Ah, newlyweds! Everything is perfect, they can do no wrong. Unless you send your husband to pick out your makeup. 

A woman named Charlema Webb shared a story about her husband asking her what she wanted as a one month wedding anniversary gift, which is adorable. She told him, but he didn't quite understand...

She writes:
