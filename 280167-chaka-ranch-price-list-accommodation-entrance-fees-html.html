Accommodation charges at this serene Park

Chaka ranch accommodation is offered in 12 luxury tents at a tented camp. The environment is quiet and welcoming giving you an opportunity to relax and unwind. This is definitely a perfect getaway with friends, family or even alone. The Chaka ranch tented camp is a walking distance from the leisure park.

The charges for Chaka ranch accommodation are as follows;

For full board rates, there are standard rooms which go for Ksh.11,500 for single, Ksh.10,000 for double and Ksh.10,00 for triple rooms. For deluxe, the charges are a bit high and for single occupancy, it is charged at Ksh.13,500, Double occupancy goes for Ksh.11,500 and triple occupancy for this type of the room is not applicable. They also have superior rooms and their charges are highest compared with the other two types of rooms. For single occupancy in the superior room, you will have to part with Ksh.15,500 and for double and triple occupancy the charges are Ksh.13,000.

The full board rates are distinguished by the 3 different room types. For a standard room, single occupancy goes for Ksh.9,000. Double and triple occupancy is charged at Ksh.7,500.
