Coping with grief

Kambua lost her father a couple of years ago, followed by her newborn son not long ago.

The losses broke her but she managed to stay strong and try to live each day at a time.

The mother of one recently shared with the world how she managed to pull through by revealing the things that she has been doing that keep her sane.

According to Kambua, reading books and travelling have been playing the perfect therapy even as she tries to heal.

'I read books and travel to heal. I have healed from things I thought would break me forever. I healed from losing my dad (I didn't think was near possible). I am healing from losing my son. It is possible to heal. The scars may remain, but God takes away the sting," Kambua said.

Adding:

''I am curious to know...what do you do to heal...? To exhale...? I read books, listen to "stuff", I pray, I travel...I travel to heal and regroup. Recently, the travel bug bit me."
