Nengi Is A Strong Lady, Ozo Reveals

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Friday, February 2, 2024

Evicted Big Brother Naija season 5 Lockdown Housemate, Ozo has spilled the tea on all he thinks about his love interest in the House, Nengi.

During his post eviction interview with BBNaija host, Ebuka Obi-Uchendu, on Monday, September 21, Ozo expressed how a great person Nengi has been to him in the House.

Excluding his drama-like representations, Ozo thinks Nengi is the best woman for him. He also remembered his friends in the House mocking him about giving birth to albino kids if her marries Nengi.

So, to him, Nengi is very pretty and attractive lady, (a complete package at that).

Ozo said; “I felt couple of times where…. on my birthday, where everyone has to talk about me and I had to say things about everyone, and I used the words ‘I feel like Nengi is a strong person,’ so that’s how it started.

“And one thing she doesn’t get is… that her attitude alone…. got me really attractive to her.

“Excluding the whole thing I think she’s a pretty and attractive lady, (a complete package for me).

“And she has always being the whole light skinned girl kind of person.

“And my friend will be like, what are you doing, you are going to give birth to albino kids. And I’ll be like… don’t worry, that’s not what will happen.”
