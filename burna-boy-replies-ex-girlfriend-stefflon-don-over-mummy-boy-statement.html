Burna Boy Replies Ex Girlfriend, Stefflon Don Over 'Mummy Boy' Statement

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

Nigerian singer, Damini Ogulu better Burna Boy, has replied British ex-lover and singer Stefflon Don after she threw a shade at ‘guys who claim to be men but are in fact, just a mama’s boy’.

Trouble had earlier started when Stefflon Don via her Tiktok handle on Thursday decided to shade guys who claim to be men but are nothing but mummy’s boys.

The video garnered a lot of mixed reactions from fans who had thought the British-Jamaican singer was referring to her ex lover, Burna Boy, who undoubtedly have a very close relationship with his mother, Bose Ogulu.

It is also worthy to note that Burna’s mother doubles as his manager as they are almost always seen together at his shows and other public events.

Replying to the statement, Burna Boy via his Instagram story on Friday, urged his ex-lover to move on and leave him be.

He wrote: ‘Move On’

Recall, Burna Boy officially ended his relationship with Stefflon Don, who he dated for some time. The couple split after three years of being together.
