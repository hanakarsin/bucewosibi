Rita Dominic, Hubby Set For White Wedding In London

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Sunday, March 24, 2024

Nollywood actress, Rita Dominic who traditionally tied the knot with her heartrob, Fidelis Anosike on April 19th,   has picked Novermber 26 for their white wedding.

It would be recalled that 46-year-old actress walked down the aisle with her Heartthrob, Fidelis Anosike on April 19th in their star studded traditional wedding in Imo state.

However, the couple is set to hold the white wedding ceremony at Carlton Towers Estate, High Street, Carlton Goole, Yorkshire, London on November, 2022.

The wedding venue is in Yorkshire North, close to Selby and Snaith train state.

According to the invitation card, hotels in the heart of Yorkshire where guest can easily lodge is 35 minutes drive from the wedding venue.

Some of the hotels close to the venue are said to be offering rooms to guests at discounted price once they quote Rita and Fidelis as booking reference.

Their wedding hashtag has been revealed to be reeldeel2022 RF.

Rita Dominic and her love met in Lagos during the 2017 Africa International Film Festival (AFRIFF) session on film business which had government representatives, business leaders and development agencies as well as media entrepreneurs and top executives

The duo made their love relationship public in December 2020 after sharing loved up picture on social media.

Fedelis Anosike, the groom is a businessman and entrepreneur. He is the chairman of the Folio Media Group, owners of Daily Times Newspaper.
