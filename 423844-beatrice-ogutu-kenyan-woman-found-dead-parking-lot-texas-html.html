Beatrice Ogutu: Kenyan Woman Found Dead at Parking Lot in Texas

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Sunday, April 7, 2024
  • The lifeless body of Beatrice Danielle Dagosi Ogutu, was found inside her car on Saturday, August 21
  • Initially, CCTV cameras had captured her walking towards her car which seemingly had mechanical problems
  • The nurse's remains were moved to a funeral parlour located in Bedford where an autopsy was conducted

A Kenyan born nurse based in the United States has died under unclear circumstances in Dallas, Texas.

The lifeless body of the medic, Beatrice Danielle Dagosi Ogutu, was found inside her car at a parking lot on Saturday, August 21.

Before her death, CCTV cameras captured her walking towards her car which seemingly had mechanical problems, Diaspora Messenger reported.

This caught the attention of the security who offered to help but she declined saying, she had phoned her friends for assistance and were on their way.

After some time, some people saw Ogutu sleeping in the car with its window lowered down a little.

After touching her, they said she was unresponsive and unsuccessfully tried to resuscitate her.

The nurse's remains were moved to a funeral parlour located in Bedford where an autopsy was conducted. However, the cause of her death is yet to be made public.

Until her death, Ogutu was a member of Truevine International Inc.

Nurse found dead in Embu

In a separate story, police launched a probe into an incident where a 38-year old woman was found dead in her house located in Kivaruka village, Embu county, under mysterious circumstances.

The body of the woman identified as Wawira Nyaga, a nurse at Kithimu Health Centre, was discovered around noon, when she failed to receive her neighbours’ calls.

According to preliminary investigations, the deceased's body did not have any visible injuries.

Her boyfriend, identified only as Munene, was found unconscious in a thicket not far from Wawira’s home.

Next to the 23-year-old man were bottles of beer and an empty processed juice can.

A reliable source privy to the matter said that the last time the couple was seen in public was on Wednesday, June 9, at around 3pm, when they visited a bar owned by the nurse.

Wawira was previously married, though she separated from her husband a few years ago. The former couple has a 10-year-old daughter.
