Two friends who were dark in Nigeria years ago now light skinned in Germany, many say abroad weather

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Saturday, July 27, 2024

- Two Nigerian men have really served a very good friendship goal with their new transformation photo taken in Munich

- In 2012, the friends were students in UNIBEN working towards bagging their degrees

- Many praised their long-term friendship, saying they really progressed in life together

Perhaps the best thing about friendship is helping each other to achieve mutual goals. Two friends seem to have done that and their story has set social media abuzz.

In a Twitter post on Tuesday, March 2, one of the friends with the handle @No4troublemaker shared a throwback photo of when they were students at the University of Benin (UNIBEN).

The second snap showed them in Munich, Germany several years later. What is obvious in both photographs was how their skins went from being dark to fair, making them look all fresh.

The change in weather probably has a lot to do with their new pigmentation. Many people were not only wowed about their new level but how they stuck with each other through the years.
