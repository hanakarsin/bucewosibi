INEC Officially Declares Winner Of Bayelsa Governorship Election

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Saturday, August 10, 2024

The Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) has officially announced the incumbent Governor of Bayelsa State and governorship candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Douye Diri, as the winner of the State governorship election held last Saturday.

Naija News reports that the Returning Officer, Prof Faruq Kuta, who is also the Vice Chancellor of the Federal University Of Technology, Minna, announced Diri winner of the poll at the collation centre of the election on Monday.

Diri polled 175,196 to defeat his closest rival, Timipre Sylva of the All Progressives Congress, who garnered 110,108 votes while the Labour Party polled 905 votes.
