TZ pastor arrested for kissing congregants apologises for misleading people, blames it on satan

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Controversial Tanzanian pastor, Tito Machibya, has come out to apologise to his congregants and countrymen claiming he is sorry for misleading them.

The pastor claimed he was being led by the devil initially but has now parted ways with him and is now being guided by the Holy Spirit.

The pastor who made the revelation in an interview with EATV admitted he misled a lot of people including his congregants with unfounded gospel before he was fully saved.

''I am saved now. You know long ago I used to mislead people. The devil used me to mislead people using false word of God. I used to use my influence to lead people to the wrong direction,'' Tito revealed.

The pastor who was recently in trouble with police claimed he is sane, only that he could not say no to the devil who convinced him to mislead his congregants and other Tanzanians.

''I am not crazy, it is just that the devil misused me. It is because of him I decided to mislead my people. However, right now I am saved and I serve a true God,'' he added.

The pastor made the revelation days after undergoing a mental check to determine if he was sane.

Tito first made headlines after he was arrested for kissing and drinking alcohol with his congregants.
