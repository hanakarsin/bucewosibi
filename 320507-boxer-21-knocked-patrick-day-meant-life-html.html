Boxer, 21, who put Patrick Day in coma clarifies he never meant for it to happen

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Thursday, April 4, 2024

A 21-year-old boxer who knocked out fellow boxer Patrick Day has spoken once again on the horrific incident in a lengthy letter.

Charles Conwell took to Twitter on Tuesday, October 22, to clarify he had absolutely no intention to harm Day in the manner that he did.

The chap further stated he was actually considering quitting boxing since the incident which cost the opponent his life.

Contwell disclosed the matter was a difficult one to discuss for both families, hence he would not be addressing the issue anymore.

“I never meant for this to happen to you. All I ever wanted to do was win. If I could take it back, I would because no one deserves this to happen to them,” he stated in the letter.

Latest reports on Day indicate he suffered severe brain damage as a result of the fight and had to be taken for emergency surgery.
