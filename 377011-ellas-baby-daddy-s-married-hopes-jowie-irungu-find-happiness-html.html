Ella Musangi's ex says he's married, hopes she and Jowie Irungu can find happiness

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

- David bin Salem had no doubt his ex-wife deserved to be loved by a good man

- He hoped she spoke to God before settling down with Jowie Irungu

- The concerned baby daddy added that he has been married for three years and Ella too deserved to find a partner who makes her happy

The baby daddy of Jowie Irungu's new flame Ella Musangi has declared jealousy is not his type of language.

The father intimately told TUKO.co.ke he has been happily married for three years and hoped his baby mama too gets to experience his type of joy.

David bin Salem revealed he has another family and still successfully and peacefully co-parents with the mother of his first child.

The concerned ex hoped that Ella had taken time to converse with God about her choice of man and prayed that Jowie was the guy who would bring light into her life.

"She too deserves that fulfilment of bringing up her own family...Let her take time to be convinced about the choice she is making," David said.

The settled husband further said he and Ella spoke right after she decided to settle down with Jowie and she seemed happy about it.

The model gave an update about their daughter and assured David she would be okay despite all the attention Jowie was getting on social media.

"Ella had called two days before the release of the video to inform me about the same and update on the child's progress," he added.

As previously reported, David said he was okay with his daughter referring to Jowie as dad because Ella and the ex-prisoner were married.

To him, the step-father who helps raise his kid deserved to feel included.

The man further explained he did not feel like his baby mama was endangering their daughter in any way.

He knew she was a good mother who had been judged harshly by the world and deserved happiness after everything she has been through.
