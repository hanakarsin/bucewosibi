Brad Mondo - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Hair stylist, entrepreneur and social media personality who is best known for his popular Hairdresser Reacts video series. He has earned over 7 million subscribers on his self-titled YouTube channel, where he posts the popular series. He is also Creative Director and CEO of his brand XMONDO, which he launched in 2019.  

Before Fame

He first began posting pictures of his work on hair to Instagram in February of 2013, and has grown in popularity from there.

Trivia

He is also a successful TikToker, and has amassed over 8 million followers on his bradmondonyc account, where he posts duets and styling videos. He is also a model, and has appeared in a variety of shoots and advertisements. 

Family Life

He was born and raised in Franklin, Massachusetts alongside his brothers Eric and John. He can often be seen with his mom on his Instagram account. He apprenticed in his father's salon growing up.  

Associated With

He has styled hair for fellow content creators such as Charli D'Amelio, Sienna Mae Gomez, Safiya Nygaard and Joana Ceddia.
