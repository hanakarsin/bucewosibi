Nkechi Blessing Reacts To Falegan's Claim Of Not Changing Her Underwear In 3 Days, Reveals Personal

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Popular Nollywood actress Nkechi Blessing has reacted to ex-lover, Opeyemi Falegan accusation of not changing her undies in 3 days.

Recall that Naija News earlier reported that Falegan had apologized to the Nollywood actress during a live session with Daddy Freeze.

Many people thought he wanted Nkechi back, hence his apology.

However, in a video sent to Uche Maduagwu, Falegan stated that was not the case and accused the actress of having poor hygiene.

In his words: “I apologized for different reasons. I apologized because I wanted bygones to be bygones. Not because I want her back..for what?

“Do you know why I left? I left for a different reason. Personal hygiene. You have to tell a woman to change her panties in three days? You have to tell a woman to brush her teeth in the morning? After the whole thing everywhere is messed up…You want me to manage that? No I wouldn’t’’

Reacting to his allegations Nkechi called him a liar while also insisting that she has not worn underpants since she was 18.
